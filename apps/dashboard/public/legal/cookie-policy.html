# Cookie Policy **Last Updated: March 29, 2026** ## 1. What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help remember your preferences and improve your experience. ## 2. How We Use Cookies ### 3.1 Authentication - Verify your identity - Maintain secure sessions - Prevent unauthorized access ### 3.2 Preferences - Remember your settings - Personalize your experience - Provide localized content ### 3.3 Analytics - Understand how you use our Service - Identify popular features - Improve user experience ### 3.4 Marketing - Deliver relevant advertisements - Measure campaign effectiveness - Track conversions ## 4. Managing Cookies ### 4.1 Cookie Consent Banner When you first visit, you'll see our cookie consent banner where you can: - Accept all cookies - Reject non-essential cookies - Customize your preferences - Access detailed cookie information ### 4.2 Browser Settings You can manage cookies through your browser: - **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies - **Firefox**: Options > Privacy > Cookies - **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies - **Edge**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies ### 4.3 Your Preferences Manage your cookie preferences anytime in Settings > Privacy > Cookies. ## 5.
